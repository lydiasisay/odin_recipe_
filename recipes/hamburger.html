Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a classic American dish that typically consists of a juicy ground beef patty, grilled to perfection and placed between two soft, round buns. It's often topped with a variety of condiments and garnishes, such as crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, tangy pickles, and melting cheese, adding layers of flavor and texture.
