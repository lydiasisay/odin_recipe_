Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of wide pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and flavorful cheeses like mozzarella and Parmesan. A typical lasagna starts with a hearty meat sauce made from ground beef or sausage, sautéed with onions, garlic, and herbs, then simmered in a tangy tomato base. Between the pasta layers, this sauce is alternated with smooth ricotta cheese mixed with eggs and herbs, creating a creamy texture. Finally, the top layer is covered with cheese, which melts and browns to a bubbly, golden perfection when baked. Serve it hot for a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sause
  • Ricotta cheese
  • shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Prepare the meat sauce
  4. Make the ricotta mixture
  5. Layer the ingredients
  6. Cover and bake
  7. Uncover and finish baking
  8. Let it rest
